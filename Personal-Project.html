Personal Project

Over the summer holiday between grade 9 and 10, Jival spent hours and hours developing a game in the Unity game creator software, that allows people to learn and create games without needing too much to create a game. For Jival's personal project, he utilised the Unity software to create his very own game in under 2 months. His game is a shooter game where you can shoot at targets from a distance to practice improving your aim in video games.
